ABOUT (pant)suit up

the alexa skill that will get you jobs.

We rely on Alexa for basic needs: to play music, to call people, to tell us the weather -- but what if Alexa could do more? More specifically, what if Alexa could help us prepare for the adult world; what if she could help us get employed?



hey, Alexa


Here's where (pant)suit up comes in: (pant)suit up is an Alexa skill that helps prepare any college-educated job hunters for any professional interview. Just say "Alexa, start my interview," and she will throw you the most commonly asked interview questions, varying in difficulty, category, and length. She gives you an opportunity to practice and rehearse your answers, and when you've finished the mock interview, Alexa will analyze what you've said and give you feedback on how it can be better. (pant)suit up is the best interview prep out there, and she will get you jobs.
